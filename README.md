DIRECTIONS
Shewing how a fit and perfect MODEL
OF
Popular Government
may be made, found, or
understood.

      * THE
FIRST MODEL
OF
POPULAR GOVERNMENT,
PROPOSED.

OF
A COMMON-WEALTH.
PROPOSED.

Present State of this Nation.
